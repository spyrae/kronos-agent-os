# Code of Conduct

KAOS follows a simple standard: be direct, technical, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent, but keep feedback concrete.
- Discuss code, behavior, security impact, and user value.
- Be clear about tradeoffs and uncertainty.
- Respect private data and do not ask contributors to share secrets publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private keys, tokens, session files, private server data, or personal workspace content.
- Encouraging abuse of agent capabilities, unauthorized access, credential theft, or evasion.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers redirect scope.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
