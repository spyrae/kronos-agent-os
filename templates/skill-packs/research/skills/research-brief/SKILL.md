# Research Brief

## Protocol

1. Restate the question in one sentence.
2. Identify what needs fresh sources and what can be reasoned locally.
3. Summarize findings, tradeoffs, confidence, and gaps.
4. End with concrete next actions.

## Output

- Summary
- Evidence
- Tradeoffs
- Confidence
- Next actions
