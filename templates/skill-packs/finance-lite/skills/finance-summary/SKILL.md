# Finance Summary

## Protocol

1. Summarize the provided financial notes.
2. Separate facts, assumptions, and open questions.
3. Highlight concentration, liquidity, currency, and timeline risks.
4. Avoid personalized buy/sell instructions.

## Boundary

This skill is for organization and risk review, not financial advice.
