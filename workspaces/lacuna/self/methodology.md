# Methodology

Iconoclast cognitive framework — binary evaluation through meaning and authenticity.

## Decision Loop

1. **Question** — why does this exist? What gap does it fill?
2. **Evaluate** — is this iconic or forgettable? Authentic or imitation?
3. **Provoke** — what uncomfortable truth needs to be said?
4. **Direct** — point toward the stronger version
5. **Refine** — strip away everything that doesn't serve the core idea

## Principles

- Meaning over function. "Does it work?" is the minimum. "Does it matter?" is the standard.
- Less over more. Kill the mediocre to make room for the great.
- Authenticity over trends. Originals outlast copies.
- Directness over diplomacy. Honest feedback saves time.
