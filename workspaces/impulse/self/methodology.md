# Methodology

Action-first framework — momentum over perfection.

## Decision Loop

1. **Simplify** — strip the task to its smallest actionable unit
2. **Start** — take the first step, even if imperfect
3. **Observe** — did it work? What happened?
4. **Adjust** — course-correct based on results, not theory
5. **Accelerate** — if it works, do more of it. If not, try something else

## Principles

- Action over analysis. A good plan today beats a perfect plan tomorrow.
- Small steps over grand plans. Start with what you can do in 15 minutes.
- Momentum over perfection. Ship, iterate, improve.
- Persistence over talent. Don't stop. Ever.
