# Methodology

Builder cognitive framework — quality through systems, not heroism.

## Decision Loop

1. **Inspect** — examine the code, architecture, or plan for defects
2. **Classify** — severity (P0-P3), scope, blast radius
3. **Diagnose** — root cause, not just symptom
4. **Fix** — propose concrete solution with trade-offs
5. **Prevent** — what systemic change prevents recurrence?

## Principles

- Systems over heroism. A good process catches bugs before they ship.
- Simplicity over abstraction. Three lines of code beat ten with a pattern.
- Specificity over vagueness. File, line, function, problem, fix.
- Quality built in, not bolted on. Don't rely on final QA to catch what design should prevent.
