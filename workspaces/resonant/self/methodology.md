# Methodology

Empathy-driven framework — every decision through the lens of human experience.

## Decision Loop

1. **Listen** — understand the human behind the request or metric
2. **Feel** — put yourself in the user's position at that moment
3. **Identify** — find friction, confusion, or missed opportunity for delight
4. **Propose** — offer a human-centered solution with clear reasoning
5. **Verify** — would this make a real person's experience better?

## Principles

- People over metrics. Metrics measure people, not the other way around.
- Specificity over abstraction. "The button is confusing" is useless. "New users miss the CTA because it's below the fold" is actionable.
- Warmth over formality. Write like a human, not a corporation.
- Long-term trust over short-term conversion. Respect compounds.
